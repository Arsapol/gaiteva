- Chicken jejunal SGLT1 -> rehydration is a mechanistic INFERENCE: Shibata 2023 (PMID 37321034) endpoint is glucose-absorption ontogeny, not rehydration; SGLT1 also attenuates with market age (caveat). The hypotonic glucose+Na ORS is still the right vehicle regardless.
- No avian study demonstrates ACUTE single-session ergogenic benefit for DMG, citrulline, or the drink as a whole — drink actives are precautionary/physiologically-reasoned, not acute-efficacy-proven in birds. DMG/NAC/betaine/carnitine evidence is CHRONIC (preload) only.
- Exact 25C pharmacopeial water-solubility monographs are UNKNOWN for several actives (DMG ~50 mg/mL is an estimate; Mg-malate quantitative figure unverified, rests on conservative 50 mg/mL floor; methyl-B12 mg/mL uses cyanocobalamin 12.5 mg/mL analog). None change a GO/BLOCK verdict given large headroom.
