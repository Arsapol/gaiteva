## Architecture: five wet liquids (S2–S5, F5) collapse into TWO DRY products + retained dry capsule

Dry-first is the master move: removing water eliminates hydrolysis, Maillard browning, microbial growth, cold chain, the photolysis of B12/P5P, the thiol-oxidation of NAC, AND the BLOCK-severity K-sorbate-at-pH-6 preservative defect — all at once. No preservative is needed in either dry SKU.

---

### PRODUCT 1 — DRY PRELOAD (feed top-dress, ~7–10 d pre-event; bird eats ~135 g feed/day)

| Ingredient | Salt/form | Dose (per kg feed) | ≈ per bird/day | Role / evidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Creatine | monohydrate | 1.0–1.5 g | ~135–200 mg | PCr/ATP chronic loading; grain diets creatine-poor (Liu 2025 PMID 39798284) |
| (alt/adjunct) GAA | guanidinoacetic acid | 0.6–1.2 g | — | arg-sparing, feed-stable; **DRY ONLY** (sol ~3.6 g/L) — never in drink |
| Betaine | anhydrous | 1.0–1.5 g | ~135–200 mg | osmolyte/heat-stress/methyl donor (Abd El-Ghany 2022 PMID 36230366); hygroscopic—desiccate |
| NAC | N-acetylcysteine | 0.3–0.5 g | ~40–68 mg | Nrf2/ascites antioxidant (PMID 41935446, 39616350, 37782109; avian drinking-water PMID via DOI 10.1080/1828051X.2024.2339466). **DRY ONLY** (thiol oxidizes wet); add last, low humidity |
| L-Glutamine | free | 5–10 g | ~0.7–1.4 g | gut-barrier/glutathione conditioning. **DRY ONLY** (deamidates to pyroglutamate+NH3 in water, PMID 10493999; Maillard w/ sugars PMID 17006601) |
| Taurine | free | 0.5 g | ~68 mg | single modest conditional dose (chickens synthesize); redox-inert |
| L-Carnitine | L-tartrate (LCLT, ~68% base) | 0.15 g LCLT (~0.1 g base) | ~14 mg base | chronic ascites/cardiac/antioxidant (Khajali 2013, 100 mg base/kg sweet-spot) |
| L-Tyrosine (optional) | free | keep ≤1.0–1.5 g | ≤100–150 mg | dry only — pI≈5.6 = solubility floor; do NOT solubilize |
| Vitamin B6 | **pyridoxine-HCl** (dry, > P5P shelf stability) | ~0.03 g | ~4 mg | **SOLE B6 source** → <15 mg/session met by construction |
| Vitamin B12 | **cyanocobalamin** (dry, > methyl- stability) | ~0.22 mg | ~30 mcg | chronic status; opaque/foil even dry |
| Trehalose | dihydrate | ~5 g | ~675 mg | non-reducing glassy-matrix stabilizer for B12/P5P/NAC + heat-stress adjunct (Yu 2025; browning 200–2000× < sucrose, O'Brien 1996 JFS DOI 10.1111/j.1365-2621.1996.tb12180.x) |
| Magnesium | feed mineral premix (MgO low-laxation, or Mg-malate) | ≤1.0 g elemental | ~135 mg | Mg lives HERE (ceiling is enteric ~0.3–0.5% feed); keep total dietary Mg <0.3% |

Mixing order: bulk carrier (rice flour) → geometric 2-step dilution of B6/B12 micro-premix → other powders → NAC last under low humidity. Pack foil + desiccant. **Shelf-life ~12–18 mo** dry, cool, opaque. No water, no preservative, no Maillard, no cold chain.

### PRODUCT 2 — FIGHT-DAY DRINK (one dry stick → 1 L cool water, make fresh, discard same day)

**FINAL-DRINK COMPOSITION (per 1 L water), all freely soluble, total solids ~6.6 g/L:**

| Ingredient | Form | g/L | Delivers |
|---|---|---|---|
| Dextrose | glucose **monohydrate** | 22.0 g | glucose **111 mM** |
| Sodium chloride | NaCl | 2.9 g | Na 49.6 mM + Cl |
| Potassium chloride | KCl | 1.1 g | K 14.8 mM + Cl |
| Sodium citrate | dihydrate (minimal buffer only) | 0.3 g | Na +3.1 mM (NOT alkali-loading) |
| Citric acid | anhydrous | 0.7 g | drops pH for taste + microbial control |
| L-Citrulline | **free** (NOT malate — avoids malate alkali) | 1.0–1.3 g | acute NO/arginine-sparing lever (~96–125 mg/session) |
| DMG | N,N-dimethylglycine (or Na-DMG) | 0.3–0.5 g | heat/oxidative support (PMID 20662970, 20666863) |

**VERIFIED KEY NUMBERS (recomputed this session):**
- glucose:Na molar = **2.11** (near SGLT1 optimum ~1.8; chicken jejunal SGLT1 Shibata 2023 PMID 37321034 — mechanistic inference, absorption-ontogeny endpoint)
- Na 52.7 mM, K 14.8 mM, Cl **64.4 mM**
- **Osmolality ~258 mOsm/kg (delivered)** — HYPOTONIC, <300 cap met, promotes net water absorption
- **DEB = +3.06 mEq/L** — NEAR-NEUTRAL, Cl-balanced (corrects old over-alkalinizing citrate stack)
- **pH ~3.8–4.2** (citric-acidified) — at this pH sorbic acid is >80% undissociated were any used, but make-fresh dry-stick needs **no preservative**
- Per session (~96 mL/2.5 kg bird): Na ~116 mg, K ~55 mg, Cl ~219 mg, **Mg 0, B6 0**, citrulline ~110 mg, DMG ~38 mg, glucose ~1.9 g

Mixing instruction (non-chemist): "Empty one stick into 1 L cool water, stir 30 s until clear, offer fresh; discard after the session / within the day." **Shelf-life: dry stick 12–18 mo; reconstituted = single fight-day, discard.**
