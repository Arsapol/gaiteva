# Substance: Creatine Monohydrate

# Substance Dossier: Creatine Monohydrate

## 1. Identity
Creatine monohydrate (N-methylguanidinoacetic acid·H2O). CAS 6020-87-7. Endogenously synthesized in birds from arginine+glycine via GAA.

## 2. Three-rule findings
- **R1 solubility:** ~13–17 g/L water (~25°C) — MODERATE; more soluble than GAA (verifier-confirmed). Dissolves slowly; higher with warmth.
- **R2 stability:** in solution slowly degrades to inactive creatinine (faster at low pH + heat) → dry storage preferred; poorer pelleting stability than GAA.
- **R3 conflicts:** acid + heat accelerate creatinine conversion; keep dry.

## 3. Avian/poultry evidence
Avian muscle responds despite endogenous synthesis (grain diets are creatine-poor). ~1200 mg/kg feed raised muscle PCr/ATP + antioxidant status in transport-stressed broilers (PMID 39798284, VERIFIED). Chronic loading.

## 4. Verdict
ADD-conditional — PRELOAD only; SUPERSEDED by [[substance-guanidinoacetic-acid-gaa]] for stability+cost. Not acute.
