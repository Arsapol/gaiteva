# Substance: Nitrate / Beetroot Extract

# Substance Dossier: Nitrate / Beetroot Extract

## 1. Identity
Inorganic nitrate (NaNO3/KNO3) and beetroot extract (nitrate source). Human ergogenic via NO/O2 economy.

## 2. Three-rule findings
- **R1 solubility:** nitrate salts highly soluble (not the issue).
- **R2 stability:** stable; but reduced to NITRITE (microbial/gut) → toxic metabolite.
- **R3 conflicts:** nitrite oxidizes hemoglobin → methemoglobin (cannot carry O2) — directly opposes the intended O2-economy benefit.

## 3. Avian/poultry evidence
HARD NEGATIVE. Avian literature is TOXICOLOGY only: methemoglobinemia + growth retardation at ~4.2 g/kg (Atef et al. 1991 PMID 1868378, VERIFIED); methemoglobinemia in laying hens from Na-nitrate (Yousefi et al. 2014 PMC4093034, VERIFIED); immunosuppression; NO donors WORSENED broiler performance. No avian ergogenic evidence exists.

## 4. Verdict
❌ BLOCK / NEVER ADD — welfare red flag (blood-O2 impairment, the opposite of intended effect). Permanent skip.
