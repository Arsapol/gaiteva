# Substance: L-Histidine

# Substance Dossier: L-Histidine

## 1. Identity
L-histidine (essential amino acid in poultry). CAS 71-00-1. Second substrate for carnosine synthesis (with β-alanine).

## 2. Three-rule findings
- **R1 solubility:** ~41 g/L water (~25°C) — OK for moderate doses.
- **R2 stability:** stable amino acid in solution; mild Maillard partner with reducing sugars.
- **R3 conflicts:** Maillard with dextrose/ribose; otherwise compatible.

## 3. Avian/poultry evidence
Co-supplementation with β-alanine maximizes broiler muscle carnosine (Qi 2021 PMC8388462). Becomes co-limiting for carnosine when β-alanine is given in excess; on its own β-alanine is the primary limiter (verifier). Chronic.

## 4. Verdict
ADD-conditional — PRELOAD only, paired with β-alanine. Ceiling ~1.3 g/kg feed is carnosine-stoichiometry-derived (OPEN: confirm whether also welfare-bounded).
