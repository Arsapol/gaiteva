# Substance: Magnesium Chloride (MgCl2)

# Substance Dossier: Magnesium Chloride (MgCl2)

## 1. Identity
Magnesium chloride (usually hexahydrate). CAS 7786-30-3 (anhyd). Soluble Mg source (chosen over MgO/glycerophosphate for solubility) + supplies Cl−.

## 2. Three-rule findings
- **R1 solubility:** ~555–560 g/L (verified; CORRECTED from 540). Very soluble. HYGROSCOPIC (store dry, sealed). At ~2 mM (~0.19 g/L) → ✅ OK. Contrast MgO ~0.0086 g/L → BLOCK.
- **R2 stability:** stable in solution; hygroscopic as solid → caking.
- **R3 conflicts:** Mg²⁺ + phosphate/carbonate → precipitate (avoid). Bitter taste (palatability). Contributes Cl− (good for DEB).

## 3. Avian/poultry evidence
Mg req ~500–600 mg/kg feed; toxicity is ENTERIC/cathartic (osmotic diarrhea, wet litter) at ~0.3–0.5% feed (3000–5000 mg/kg), NOT a blood-Mg neuromuscular ceiling; Cl ameliorates. At ~2 mM in drink delivered Mg is tiny (<15 mg at 300 mL) → well under laxative threshold.

## 4. Verdict
ADD-conditional (minimal dose) — FIGHT-DAY trace Mg + Cl balance. Keep summed elemental Mg <150 mg/session. Specify chloride salt.
