# Substance: D-Ribose

# Substance Dossier: D-Ribose

## 1. Identity
D-ribose (aldopentose). CAS 50-69-1. Reducing pentose; marketed for ATP/recovery.

## 2. Three-rule findings
- **R1 solubility:** very soluble (~1000+ g/L). Not a solubility issue.
- **R2 stability:** REDUCING SUGAR — strong Maillard browning with amines (worse than glucose); browns drinks containing amino acids/B6 → potency/color loss.
- **R3 conflicts:** Maillard with amino acids/taurine/B6; high osmotic load at the 20 g/100mL it appeared at in old S2.

## 3. Avian/poultry evidence
NO peer-reviewed avian SKELETAL-MUSCLE performance/recovery evidence (verifier: absence confirmed across searches). Only Tier-3 patent claims for cardiac/ascites. Even human ergogenic evidence is weak. Not a fast carb (slow, non-glycemic).

## 4. Verdict
SKIP / REMOVE from all products — no avian basis = wasted cost + Maillard/osmotic liability. Use [[substance-dextrose]] for fast carb instead.
