# Substance: Sodium Chloride (NaCl)

# Substance Dossier: Sodium Chloride (NaCl)

## 1. Identity
Sodium chloride. CAS 7647-14-5. Source of Na+ (SGLT1 + DEB) and Cl− (DEB balance, prevents alkalosis drift).

## 2. Three-rule findings
- **R1 solubility:** ~360 g/L (25°C, verified). At 2.9 g/L → ratio ~124× → ✅ OK.
- **R2 stability:** fully stable.
- **R3 conflicts:** none chemical. Contributes BOTH Na+ and Cl− → unlike Na-citrate it does NOT alkalinize (Cl balances DEB). Adds to total Na budget.

## 3. Avian/poultry evidence
NRC broiler Na req ~0.20%, Cl ~0.23%; practical salt ceiling ~0.5% (excess → polydipsia/wet litter). DEB (Na+K−Cl) optimal ~150–300 mEq/kg; NaCl is DEB-neutral. Na excreted renally (uricotelic bird) — moderate excess cleared, but acute Na overload + dehydration is a real risk; keep total Na visible.

## 4. Verdict
ADD-strong — FIGHT-DAY: SGLT1 Na source + Cl for near-neutral DEB. Replaces alkalinizing citrate as the K/electrolyte-carrier rationale.
