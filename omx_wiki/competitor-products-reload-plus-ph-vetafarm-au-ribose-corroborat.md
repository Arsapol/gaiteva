# Competitor Products — RELOAD PLUS (PH) + Vetafarm (AU)

Two independent commercial avian/poultry lines researched 2026-06-16 for comparison against [[gamefowl-recovery-hydration-formula-vnext2-two-dry-sku-redesign]]. Both **corroborate D-ribose as an accepted avian recovery ingredient** (supports the operator's >500-test field evidence; see [[substance-d-ribose]]).

## RELOAD PLUS (Philippines)
- **Maker:** Excellence Poultry and Livestock Specialist, Inc. ("Tatak Excellence"), Quezon City. PH vet reg **VRM 16-2587**. Distributors: RGLEX Agrivet (PH), Atlantic Coast (US export).
- **Composition (label, retailer scan):** D-Ribose + Potassium Chloride + Sodium Citrate. NOTE: printed "400 g / 12 g / 6 g per mL" is a transcription error (physically impossible) — almost certainly mg/mL or per-bottle; **concentrations UNVERIFIED**. No vitamins/amino acids/sugars on the confirmed label. (gamefowlsupplement.com's "Levocarnitine" claim = cross-product confusion with sister SKU RELOAD MAX.)
- **Claim:** "The Stress Buster" — energy, travel/transport-stress recovery, rehydration, ATP replenishment. Oral DROPS (not injectable).
- **Dose:** conditioning 7 drops AM+PM; pre-basket 7 drops; post-transport rest 30-45 min then 7 drops; chicks 2 mL / 20 mL water.
- **Sister product RELOAD MAX:** D-Ribose + Glucose + L-Carnitine, energy/pointing focus, 7 drops q2h, final 1 h pre-fight.

## Vetafarm (Australia) — avian vet company
- **Spark Liquid (avian / all-animals):** Glucose **135 g/L**, citrate 30.31 g/L, Na 13–21 g/L (source discrepancy: official page 13, retailer label scans 21 + bicarbonate 23 g/L), K 20 g/L. Chloride + osmolality UNKNOWN. Dose **20 mL/L** drinking water, fresh daily. At dilution: glucose ~15 mmol/L, Na ~18, K ~10, citrate ~3, bicarb ~7.5 mmol/L.
- **Pigeon Ribolyte (DISCONTINUED):** **Ribose 330 g/L** + glucose + Na/K/citrate (electrolyte amounts unpublished). Dose 10 mL/100 mL, before+after racing. → replaced by **Pigeon Recharge** = **Ribose + L-Carnitine**.
- **Poly Aid Plus** (powder, sick-bird crop feed): maize starch + albumen + NaCl/KCl/MgSO4/Ca-gluconate + full vitamin premix + probiotic. Recovery-feed, not an ORS.
- **Synbiotic:** 9-strain probiotic + inulin + enzymes. Gut, not rehydration.

## What this tells us for our formula
1. **Ribose is mainstream in avian recovery products** — PH sabong (RELOAD PLUS, RELOAD MAX) AND AU pigeon-racing by a vet company (Ribolyte 330 g/L, Recharge). Commercial Tier-2/3 corroboration of the operator's field result. Reinforces KEEP ribose.
2. **Ribose + glucose coexist commercially** (RELOAD MAX, Ribolyte) — supports keeping ribose alongside dextrose in our carbohydrate/energy phase, separate from the amino-acid product.
3. **Ribose + L-carnitine pairing** (RELOAD MAX, Pigeon Recharge) mirrors our preload carnitine — independent convergence.
4. **Our SGLT1 drink is stronger than Spark:** Spark delivers only ~15 mmol/L glucose at label dilution (palatability/mild energy), well below SGLT1-optimal ~40–140 mmol/L. Our drink = 111 mmol/L glucose + 50 mM Na → genuine SGLT1 co-transport. Advantage: ours.
5. **Both competitors lean alkalinizing** (citrate ± bicarbonate, no declared chloride) — same human-ORS habit our avian reframe flags. Our near-neutral Cl-balanced DEB (+3 mEq/L) is the deliberate differentiator; verify it's actually superior in a panting bird (still an open inference).
6. **Gap none of them fill:** a chronic DRY PRELOAD (creatine/GAA + betaine + NAC). All three are acute liquids only — our preload SKU is a genuine whitespace.
