# Substance: Dextrose (D-Glucose)

# Substance Dossier: Dextrose (D-Glucose)

## 1. Identity
D-glucose monohydrate (dextrose). CAS 5996-10-1. Reducing monosaccharide; SGLT1 substrate.

## 2. Three-rule findings
- **R1 solubility:** ~900 g/L (~91 g/100mL, 25°C). At 16.2 g/L drink → ratio ~55× → ✅ OK.
- **R2 stability:** stable in solution; REDUCING SUGAR → Maillard browning with amines (amino acids/B6) over storage; sugar+warmth = microbial growth → fight-day drink make-fresh.
- **R3 conflicts:** Maillard with β-alanine/histidine/taurine/B6 (keep amino acids out of dextrose drink); fine with betaine (no free amine).

## 3. Avian/poultry evidence
Drives active water uptake via intestinal SGLT1 glucose+Na co-transport (Shibata 2023 PMID 37321034, VERIFIED — CAVEAT: SGLT1 functional in young birds, ATTENUATED by ~market age 5wk; state age dependence). 4% glucose water stabilized blood viscosity/hematocrit/osmolality under heat (Zhou 1998 PMID 9603349, VERIFIED). Birds have naturally high diet-insensitive blood glucose → value is hydration/osmolality, not a glucose "spike".

## 4. Verdict
ADD-strong — FIGHT-DAY drink backbone. Pair with Na at glucose:Na molar ~1-2:1 for SGLT1.
