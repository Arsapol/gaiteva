# Gamefowl Capsule Restructure vNext2 — Function-Split, Tyrosine-Forward Focus Capsule

# Capsule Restructure vNext2

The legacy 450 mg capsule (variants A/B/C) mixed THREE incompatible jobs. New mechanism knowledge ([[substance-l-tyrosine]], [[substance-l-phenylalanine]], [[substance-l-glutamine]]) forces a function-split.

## Diagnosis of the legacy capsule
| Ingredient | Legacy role | New verdict | Where it belongs |
|---|---|---|---|
| L-Tyrosine 50-110 | "focus" | ✅ THE focus/dopamine lever, but stress-gated + likely under-dosed | ACUTE focus capsule, raise dose |
| L-Phenylalanine 10-60 | "focus" | ❌ redundant w/ tyrosine + competes at BBB (as L-isomer) | DROP |
| Rhodiola 10-50 | "adaptogen" | ❌ Tier-3, no avian basis | DROP |
| P5P (B6) 10 | cofactor | ✅ AADC cofactor (L-DOPA→dopamine) | KEEP in focus capsule (watch B6 cap) |
| L-Glutamine 70-100 | "recovery" | ⚠️ not focus; gut/anti-fatigue, BBB-buffered | MOVE to dry PRELOAD |
| Creatine 30-130 | "power" | ⚠️ chronic PCr loading, useless acute | MOVE to dry PRELOAD |
| NAC 60-90 | "antioxidant" | ⚠️ chronic Nrf2/ascites antioxidant | MOVE to dry PRELOAD |
| Mg-glycerophosphate 60-80 | Mg | ⚠️ Mg belongs in feed/preload (enteric ceiling) | MOVE to PRELOAD/feed |

Net: only Tyrosine + B6 are true ACUTE focus ingredients. Everything else is chronic preload or dead weight.

## NEW — ACUTE FOCUS CAPSULE (give ~45-60 min pre-fight, relatively empty crop)
Completes the FULL dopamine-synthesis cofactor chain (legacy capsule was missing the DBH cofactors):

| Ingredient | Dose / capsule | Why |
|---|---|---|
| L-Tyrosine | ~250-300 mg | direct dopamine/NE precursor; works under fight-stress (TH de-inhibited). Raised from legacy 50-110 (likely sub-threshold). AVIAN OPTIMUM UNKNOWN — conservative, uricotelic clearance makes modest excess safe. |
| Pyridoxal-5-P (B6) | 5 mg | AADC cofactor (L-DOPA→dopamine). COUNT toward <15 mg B6/session total. |
| Vitamin C (Na-ascorbate or ascorbic) | 25-50 mg | DBH cofactor (dopamine→norepinephrine) + anti-stress; legacy capsule HAD NONE. Birds synthesize but heat/fight-stress depletes (Edens). |
| Copper (trace, e.g. Cu-gluconate) | 0.1-0.3 mg | DBH cofactor; legacy had none. Keep tiny (trace vs feed Cu ~8 mg/kg). |
| (Iron for TH) | none added | TH needs Fe2+ but diet supplies it; do not add. |

~300 mg fill — one capsule.

### Why give it right and timed
- **Fasted-ish:** tyrosine competes with other large amino acids at the brain barrier (LAT1) → give away from a high-protein crop-fill or brain uptake drops.
- **Pre-fight ~1 h:** human onset ~1 h, lasts ~3 h; covers a multi-round bout.
- **Stress-gated:** does nothing on a calm resting bird — that's expected, not failure.

## Formula-safety-check (DRY capsule)
- R1 water-solubility: N/A as gate — capsule dissolves in crop/gut, not a pre-made solution. Tyrosine low water-solubility does NOT matter dry.
- R2 stability: dry = stable; foil + desiccant.
- R3 compatibility: dry = no Maillard/redox. NOTE: vitamin C + copper would redox-cycle IN SOLUTION, but in a DRY capsule they are fine; do NOT pre-dissolve this capsule in water and store.
- Caps: B6 (this 5 mg + preload + any drink) < 15 mg/session. Copper trace, far below tolerance.

## What moved to the dry PRELOAD ([[gamefowl-recovery-hydration-formula-vnext2-two-dry-sku-redesign]])
Creatine/GAA, L-glutamine, NAC, Mg — all chronic, all already in the preload SKU. No loss, correct timing.

## Open items
- Avian tyrosine dose-response UNKNOWN — 250-300 mg is reasoned (human ~100-150 mg/kg under stress; 2.5 kg bird) not avian-validated. Field-titrate.
- If keeping the A/B/C "striker/endurance/power" identities: FOCUS = this acute capsule; ENDURANCE/POWER are really PRELOAD goals (creatine dose), not separate acute pills.
- Peer-review (codex) the copper addition + tyrosine dose before locking.

## Copper source LOCKED 2026-06-16 — Tribasic Copper Chloride (TBCC)

The capsule's trace copper (DBH cofactor) = **TBCC, Cu2(OH)3Cl, ~58% Cu**. Chosen specifically because it is co-formulated with vitamin C: TBCC releases Cu2+ slowly in the dry matrix and is the poultry-premix benchmark for NOT destroying co-formulated vitamins (PMID 15971525), vs copper sulfate which would oxidize the ascorbate. Bioavailability ~109% of CuSO4 in broilers; EFSA-approved.

- **Dose:** 0.3 mg elemental Cu = **0.52 mg TBCC** (0.1 mg Cu = 0.17 mg; 0.2 mg = 0.34 mg).
- **Safety:** ~7.5 mg/kg-feed-equiv vs NRC MTL ~300 mg/kg = ~40x margin. Trivially safe.
- **2nd choice:** Cu bis-glycinate (~30% Cu). **Rejected:** CuSO4 (pro-oxidant, destroys vit C), CuO (~0% avian bioavailability), Cu-gluconate (low %Cu, thin avian data).
- **R3 reminder:** Cu + ascorbate redox needs liquid water → safe DRY only (<40% RH, desiccant); never pre-dissolve. Keep NAC out of this capsule (Cu-thiolate risk).

See [[substance-copper-trace-dbh-cofactor]]. Updated capsule copper row: "Copper (TBCC, ~58% Cu) 0.17-0.52 mg compound → 0.1-0.3 mg elemental Cu".
